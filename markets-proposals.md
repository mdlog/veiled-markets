# Veiled Markets — Market Proposals

> Daftar market baru untuk di-deploy. Campuran binary (2 outcomes) dan multi-outcome (3–4 outcomes).
> Tanggal acuan: Maret 2026.

## CRYPTO (Category: 3)

### 1. Will Bitcoin exceed $150,000 by June 30, 2026?
- **Type:** Binary (2 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Yes / No
- **Description:** Resolves YES if the price of Bitcoin (BTC/USD) reaches or exceeds $150,000 at any point before June 30, 2026 23:59 UTC, as reported by CoinGecko. Resolves NO otherwise.
- **Resolution Source:** https://www.coingecko.com/en/coins/bitcoin
- **Deadline:** June 30, 2026
- **Token:** ALEO

### 2. What will Ethereum's price range be on July 1, 2026?
- **Type:** 4-way (4 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Below $3,000 / $3,000–$5,000 / $5,000–$8,000 / Above $8,000
- **Description:** Resolves based on the CoinGecko closing price of Ethereum (ETH/USD) on July 1, 2026 at 00:00 UTC. The outcome matching the price range at that exact snapshot wins.
- **Resolution Source:** https://www.coingecko.com/en/coins/ethereum
- **Deadline:** July 1, 2026
- **Token:** ALEO

### 3. Will Solana flip Ethereum in daily DEX volume before Q4 2026?
- **Type:** Binary (2 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Yes / No
- **Description:** Resolves YES if Solana's total daily DEX trading volume exceeds Ethereum's (L1 only, excluding L2s) for at least 7 consecutive days before October 1, 2026, as reported by DefiLlama. Resolves NO otherwise.
- **Resolution Source:** https://defillama.com/dexs/chains
- **Deadline:** September 30, 2026
- **Token:** USDCX

### 4. Which blockchain will have the highest TVL on September 1, 2026?
- **Type:** 4-way (4 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Ethereum / Solana / BNB Chain / Other
- **Description:** Resolves to the blockchain with the highest Total Value Locked (TVL) on September 1, 2026 at 00:00 UTC, as reported by DefiLlama. "Other" covers any chain not listed (Tron, Arbitrum, etc.). L2s are counted separately from Ethereum L1.
- **Resolution Source:** https://defillama.com/chains
- **Deadline:** September 1, 2026
- **Token:** ALEO

### 5. Will Aleo mainnet token price reach $5 before August 2026?
- **Type:** Binary (2 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Yes / No
- **Description:** Resolves YES if the ALEO token price reaches or exceeds $5.00 USD on any major exchange (as listed on CoinGecko) at any point before August 1, 2026 00:00 UTC. Resolves NO otherwise.
- **Resolution Source:** https://www.coingecko.com/en/coins/aleo
- **Deadline:** July 31, 2026
- **Token:** ALEO

## POLITICS (Category: 1)

### 6. Will the US pass a federal crypto regulatory framework before 2027?
- **Type:** Binary (2 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Yes / No
- **Description:** Resolves YES if a comprehensive federal cryptocurrency regulatory bill is signed into law by the US President before January 1, 2027. Executive orders alone do not count. The bill must address exchange regulation, stablecoin oversight, or token classification. Resolves NO otherwise.
- **Resolution Source:** https://www.congress.gov/
- **Deadline:** December 31, 2026
- **Token:** USDCX

### 7. Who will lead in US 2028 presidential primary polls by end of 2026?
- **Type:** 3-way (3 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Republican Frontrunner / Democrat Frontrunner / No Clear Leader
- **Description:** Resolves based on the RealClearPolitics polling average on December 31, 2026. "Republican Frontrunner" wins if a Republican candidate leads overall. "Democrat Frontrunner" wins if a Democrat leads. "No Clear Leader" wins if the margin between the top two candidates from different parties is less than 2 percentage points.
- **Resolution Source:** https://www.realclearpolling.com/
- **Deadline:** December 31, 2026
- **Token:** ALEO

### 8. Will the EU implement MiCA enforcement actions against a major exchange in 2026?
- **Type:** Binary (2 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Yes / No
- **Description:** Resolves YES if any EU member state or the European Securities and Markets Authority (ESMA) takes formal enforcement action (fine, license revocation, or cease-and-desist) against a top-20 crypto exchange (by CoinGecko volume) under MiCA regulation before December 31, 2026. Resolves NO otherwise.
- **Resolution Source:** https://www.esma.europa.eu/
- **Deadline:** December 31, 2026
- **Token:** USDCX

## CLIMATE (Category: 8)

### 20. Will 2026 be the hottest year on record globally?
- **Type:** Binary (2 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Yes / No
- **Description:** Resolves YES if 2026 is declared the hottest year on record by NASA GISS or NOAA in their annual global temperature report (typically published January 2027). Resolves NO otherwise.
- **Resolution Source:** https://data.giss.nasa.gov/gistemp/ / https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/global-temperature-anomalies/
- **Deadline:** January 31, 2027
- **Token:** ALEO

### 21. Which region will experience the most severe natural disaster impact in 2026?
- **Type:** 3-way (3 outcomes)
- **Outcomes:** Asia-Pacific / Americas / Europe-Africa
- **Description:** Resolves based on the region with the highest total economic damage from natural disasters in 2026, as reported by the EM-DAT International Disaster Database or Munich Re's annual natural catastrophe review. If the report is not published by March 2027, resolves based on the latest available EM-DAT data.
- **Resolution Source:** https://www.emdat.be/ / https://www.munichre.com/en/solutions/for-industry-clients/natcatservice.html
- **Deadline:** March 31, 2027
- **Token:** USDCX
